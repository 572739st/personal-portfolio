Semester 3 · Film · Trailer
Video Production · Editing
Album Trailer OV 09

A cinematic album trailer for OV 09, built with visual effects, pacing, and audio-driven storytelling to showcase the project’s visual identity.

This project is an album trailer created to promote Young Vlogga’s upcoming album OV 09. The concept is deeply personal and cinematic, focusing on a traumatic childhood experience that shaped who he is today. Instead of a standard promotional video, the goal was to create an emotional narrative that connects the audience to the artist on a human level.

The trailer recreates a real-life event from when he was around 10 years old, involving a violent home robbery on Christmas Day. This moment became a defining point in his life and heavily influenced his identity and artistic direction.

My role in this project included directing, script development, producing, camera operation, sound design, and video editing. The main challenge was translating a sensitive real-life memory into a cinematic story while maintaining realism, emotional depth, and respect for the subject matter.
