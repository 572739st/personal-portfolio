I'm a creative at Fontys ICT who codes, designs, films, and brands. My work doesn't sit neatly in one box — I've built games in JavaScript, mobile apps in Flutter, full-stack webapps in Python, and brand identities from scratch.

"Show, don't tell — I let the work speak."

Over three semesters I've worked with real clients — a DJ/producer with zero branding, a healthcare platform (CZ Zorgvinder). I've also made things purely because I was immersed in it: a rap streetwear deep-dive, a travel guide webapp.

This portfolio is an honest record of my journey.
